Supporting MMA Gyms through the Pandemic

MMA Gyms are struggling accross the world due to lockdowns and restrictions preventing professional and hobbyist fighters from training. Gym owners find no newcomers and slowly lose members who struggle with the monthly membership fees.

The MMA Stake Pool exists to give financial support to independent gyms through ADA delegation and staking.

Funding Talent

Young talented athletes almost always struggle financially before reaching their potential and obtaining contracts with larger organisations.
Once the pandemic settles and gyms are able to stay afloat on their own, the MMA Stake Pool will gradually switch to supporting talented fighters in need of better financial stability. Your delegation will directly help talented fighters achieve their dreams.

Current Situation

The MMA Stake Pool is currently supporting Sanefighting Gym in Munich.

While the pool is still in its infancy, fees will stay low and rewards will be used to grow the pledge.

Independent MMA or BJJ gyms in lockdown are welcome to contact the MMA Stake Pool for support.
Aspiring professional fighters struggling to make ends meet are just as welcome.

Specifications and Architecture

The MMA Stake Pool runs on Microsoft Azure's cloud services providing connections all around the world. In addition to standard server security, Azure provides extra security services to ensure the safety and stability of the pool. Security specifics are skipped over to limit outside knowledge.

The servers all have the hardware specifications recommended by the Cardano Foundation

Remember, your ADA is never at risk because of the pool you choose when delegating. The only way for you to lose your ADA is through your 24-word phrase or sending it to the wrong place from your wallet.
